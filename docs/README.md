## Submission Summary

**Project:** CareerOS — Multi-Agent Career Intelligence Platform
**Track:** Reasoning Agents
**Pitch:**

> CareerOS is a multi-agent reasoning platform that researches your target
> role, builds a personalized Career Twin showing who you are now vs. who
> you'll become, identifies skill gaps with clear explanations, generates a
> strategic learning roadmap and portfolio plan, and explains every single
> recommendation through a transparent, visible agent reasoning trace.
