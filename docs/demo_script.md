# CareerOS — Demo Script (3–4 minutes)

## 0:00 – 0:15 | Landing & Career Journey Map
- Open landing page.
- Point at the Career Journey Map: "This is the full journey CareerOS takes
  you on — from where you are today to your target role."

## 0:15 – 0:45 | Resume Upload
- Click "Get Started" → Upload page.
- Upload a real resume PDF.
- Show the Resume Intelligence Agent's extracted skills/projects appear
  (briefly mention confidence score).

## 0:45 – 1:05 | Configure
- Enter target role: e.g. "AI Engineer"
- Set time availability slider: e.g. 10 hrs/week
- Click "Generate My Career Plan"

## 1:05 – 1:30 | Agent Pipeline (narrate while loading)
- "Right now, 9 agents are running sequentially — each one passes its
  output as context to the next. This isn't a single prompt, it's a real
  multi-step reasoning chain."

## 1:30 – 1:50 | Readiness Score
- Show Career Readiness Score: e.g. 42% → 58% → 79% → 92%
- Point out the methodology explanation and "Key Levers" section.

## 1:50 – 2:20 | Career Twin
- Show Current You vs Future You comparison.
- Highlight the transformation_highlights list.

## 2:20 – 2:50 | Skill Gap & Roadmap
- Scroll to Skill Gap Analysis — point out priority badges and
  "Why CareerOS recommends this" explanations.
- Scroll to Roadmap Timeline — show week-by-week breakdown.

## 2:50 – 3:20 | Projects & Interview Prep
- Show ranked project recommendations with relevance explanations.
- Switch tabs in Interview Prep panel (HR / Technical / Project-based).

## 3:20 – 3:50 | Agent Reasoning Trace (closing highlight)
- Scroll to the bottom: Agent Reasoning Trace.
- "Every single agent's reasoning, confidence, and assumptions are fully
  visible — nothing is a black box."

## 3:50 – 4:00 | Close
- "CareerOS — built on Microsoft AI Foundry and Azure OpenAI. Thank you."
